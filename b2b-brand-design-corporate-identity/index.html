How Does It Work?

Proven processes and timelines.

Your brand engagement is delivered through a defined operating model built around agreed scopes that emphasise clarity, control, and accountability. Delivery is coordinated by a dedicated account lead, supported by specialist strategy and design teams. Progress is reviewed through structured checkpoints to ensure alignment, quality, and consistency.

  1. 1

    Discovery and Brand Assessment

    Your engagement begins with a discovery of your brand assets, messaging, and competitive positioning, in order to identify commercially meaningful gaps and opportunities that affect credibility, sales, and operations.

  2. 2

    Strategy and Architecture

    Discovery insights are used to define a clear brand narrative and strategy, including positioning and brand architecture. We then agree on a practical framework and roadmap to guide decisions, align stakeholders, and ensure execution consistency across markets.

  3. 3

    Corporate Identity Design

    Approved strategy is translated into a practical identity system, including visual standards and usage rules that can be consistently applied by internal teams, partners, and vendors.

  4. 4

    Brand Implementation and Rollout

    Your brand system is applied across touchpoints, using a staged approach that prioritizes sales and operations. Brand systems are reviewed and refined to ensure they remain effective as the organisation evolves.

Your Brand Delivery Team

Who’s in your branding team?

Your corporate brand strategy is led by VIMI’s senior management and delivered by a specialist team that supports execution, clarity, accountability, and consistency. Your brand delivery team may include the following roles:

Project Manager

Your Project Manager serves as your primary point of contact throughout the engagement. This role manages timelines, coordinates communication, consolidates feedback, and ensures the project progresses with clarity, control, and alignment against agreed deliverables.

Brand Designer

Your Brand Designer translates approved strategy into a clear and credible visual identity. This includes the design of core brand assets, visual systems, and practical identity standards that support recognition, consistency, and professional presentation across touchpoints.

Messaging Copywriter

Your Messaging Specialist helps define how your business communicates its value with clarity and consistency. This role supports the development of core messaging, brand language, and written content that strengthens credibility with customers, investors, partners, and employees.

Managing Partner Oversight

Senior oversight is provided at key stages of the engagement to maintain quality, strategic alignment, and executive accountability. This ensures the final output remains commercially grounded, consistent with your objectives, and suitable for use in high-trust B2B environments.

FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

Straight answers to the questions B2B buyers actually ask about brand design.

Schedule a Call with Mike Darnell →
Why should a company invest in brand design if sales are the immediate priority?

Your company should invest in brand design because brand clarity affects commercial efficiency. When you’re selling complex and high-trust solutions, your brand helps buyers understand value faster, supports better pricing, and reduces friction in long sales cycles.

What is included in VIMI’s brand service?

The service is structured to provide you with corporate brand strategy, brand identity design, and brand portfolio design. This means:

  • Clarifying your positioning and narrative
  • Translating them into a credible visual identity system
  • Organizing brands, sub-brands, and offerings into a coherent structure stakeholders can understand and apply consistently.
Is this mainly a logo project, or something broader?

Your project scope can be limited to creating your visual identity, however you stand to gain better ROI when your project goal is stated to be the creation of a practical brand system that supports sales, operations, investor communication, and market consistency.

How does VIMI approach a branding engagement?

Your project is structured so that strategy comes first, design follows approved direction, and rollout is staged around your commercial priorities, with checkpoints at the start and finish of each phase:

  • Discovery and brand assessment
  • Strategy and architecture
  • Corporate identity design
  • Implementation and rollout
Who from VIMI is involved in a brand project?

Your project team is led by an assigned VIMI project manager, who directs a team that includes brand designers and messaging copywriters. The team operates with close oversight by our managing partners.

How do we know the brand work will be commercially useful, not just visually attractive?

The emphasis for your project is on building a brand that supports your credibility, pricing power, investor confidence, and talent attraction. Implementation is prioritized across your most critical sales and operations touchpoints first, so as to deliver as much ROI as possible early on.
