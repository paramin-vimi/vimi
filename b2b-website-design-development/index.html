How Does It Work?

Proven processes and timelines.

Your project is delivered through a defined operating model built around agreed scope, clear checkpoints, and accountable communication. Delivery is coordinated by a dedicated project lead, with senior oversight to maintain quality and commercial alignment.

  1. 1

    Discovery and Benchmarking

    Your website project begins with a structured review of your goals, current website, user needs, and relevant opportunities and benchmarks. Together, we establish what the website needs to achieve and what are the most meaningful opportunities for improvements.

  2. 2

    Strategy, Sitemap, and Experience Design

    Discovery insights are translated into a practical website plan. This includes sitemap validation, wireframes, content mapping, user journeys, and page-design direction so all stakeholders can align before development begins.

  3. 3

    Content, Development, and QA

    Once direction is approved, VIMI prepares content, builds the website, applies integrations, and tests the platform against agreed requirements, content, and design standards.

  4. 4

    Launch, Training, and Ongoing Support

    Once you give your approval, your website is deployed to hosting and handed over to your team with training and documentation. Warranty and maintenance options help keep your website stable and current post-launch.

Your Website Delivery Team

Who’s in your website project team?

Your website engagement is led by VIMI’s senior management and delivered by a specialist team structured to give you reliable progress, consistent quality control, and clear communication throughout the project. Depending on your project scope VIMI’s delivery team may include the following roles.

Project Lead

Your Project Lead is your main point of contact and the person responsible for keeping delivery on track. They coordinate communication, manage timelines, gather feedback, and ensure the work remains aligned to the agreed scope. Your project lead will provide you with weekly progress reports and will always be available via email and messaging.

Information Architect / UX Strategist

This role, often undertaken by one of our managing partners, helps structure your sitemap, page hierarchy, and content flow to ensure your website provides value to your stakeholders and supports your company’s revenue growth.

UX/UI Designer

Your designer translates approved goals and structure into a clear, credible interface that supports both usability and brand perception.

B2B Copywriter

Your copywriter develops messaging that explains your offer in language professional buyers understand, helping the website communicate value clearly and commercially.

Developers

Your developers build the approved website, manage technical implementation, apply integrations, and support QA so the final output is stable, secure, and suitable for launch.

Managing Partner

Senior oversight is provided at key stages of the website engagement to maintain quality, strategic alignment, and executive accountability. This helps ensure the final website remains commercially grounded, aligned with your business objectives, and suitable for high-trust B2B buying environments.

FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

Straight answers to the questions B2B buyers actually ask about website projects.

How is VIMI different from a typical website agency?

VIMI specializes in supporting B2B organizations that need help with their website strategy, copy, UX/UI, and development. Our clients enjoy accountable project delivery within an agreed scope, with senior oversight and clear communication throughout.

What business outcomes should a new website support?

Your new website should support your organization’s credibility, facilitate stakeholder understanding of your capabilities and offers, and deliver a consistent and predictable flow of qualified leads for your company.

What is included in the website project?

Your website project includes strategy, sitemap planning, UX/UI design, copywriting, content upload, development, testing, and deployment. You may also request support with integrating your website into your existing workflows and platforms.

How long does a website project usually take?

The timeline depends on the scope of your project. That said, we recommend a phased rollout that prioritizes launching your new homepage as early as possible. In most cases, the homepage attracts the greatest share of traffic and delivers the strongest commercial impact, so launching it early allows your business to capture a substantial share of the project’s ROI early on.

Can VIMI help with copywriting and content preparation?

Yes, our team is geared to support your website project with copywriting, image creation, video, and broader content preparation. Projects often move faster when content creation is handled as part of the engagement.

Can our team manage the website after launch?

Yes, your team can manage day-to-day updates of your website independently after launch. Your project includes delivery of the necessary training and documentation to facilitate this.

What happens after launch?

Your website comes with a lifetime bug-fix warranty, with optional maintenance packages available for platform updates, content changes, hosting support, and future enhancements.
