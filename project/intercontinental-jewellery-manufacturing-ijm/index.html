Intercontinental Jewellery Manufacturing (IJM)

Unique Value Proposition

IJM combines mass-market scale — operating at 80,000 to 250,000 pieces per month — with the technology base required for above-ordinary engineering ingenuity, including robotic setting, continuous casting, mass finishing, and 3D printing. Duty Free Zone status simplifies materials importation and cross-border logistics, and the firm’s certification profile provides a defensible answer to the procurement and ESG diligence requirements of international brand buyers.

The Project — Scope of Work

VIMI was engaged to design and deliver IJM’s corporate website. The scope covered:

  • UX and UI design suitable for senior B2B buyers evaluating IJM alongside other shortlisted manufacturers.
  • Development on a content management platform that allows IJM’s in-house team to update product imagery, certifications, and technical content independently.
  • Integration of b2blead.ai for inquiry capture.
