About Us

Bangkok web design, apps & digital marketing.

Our mission since 2010 has been to create success for “Invisible Giants” — industry, manufacturing & finance B2B companies whose activities shape our daily lives, but are unknown to the general public.

Our vision is well-being through benign business. We are proudly both Thai and International.

Vimi.co was founded in Bangkok in 2010 by directors Virode Imtarnasan and Mike Darnell. The two were introduced by a mutual friend and initially worked together as part of a team at a technology company focused on mobile technologies.

Between them, they have accumulated more than 50 years of experience in setting up and operating high-performance online platforms for Fortune 1,000 companies, established SMEs, and nascent startups.

How We Do It

Our process follows these principles.

Behavioral Design

People love platforms that account for how they feel & think. These are the insights behavioral design provides.

User Testing

The compass ensuring a platform’s utility & appeal is the study of the people using it. The sooner this study is launched, the higher the returns it delivers.

Return On Investment

A platform’s success is measured in relation to quantifiable goals. Failure can be useful, when we learn from it.
