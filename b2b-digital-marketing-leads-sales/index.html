How Does It Work?

Proven processes and timelines.

Your project is delivered through a defined operating model built around agreed monthly scopes that emphasise clarity, control, and accountability. Execution is coordinated weekly by a dedicated account lead, supported by specialist teams. Progress is reviewed through scheduled monthly reporting and review sessions.

  1. 1

    Discovery and Benchmarking

    Your engagement begins with a structured discovery phase to establish a clear baseline for your website and current marketing performance. We use our proprietary AI empowered platform, to assess traffic, inquiry quality, and lead generation to identify where improvements are possible and commercially meaningful.

  2. 2

    Strategy and Planning

    Discovery insights are used to define realistic, outcome-driven benchmarks aligned with pipeline development, revenue contribution, and long-term growth. Together, we agree on a clear plan and timeline that prioritises opportunities across short-, mid-, and long-term horizons.

  3. 3

    Deployment

    Once the strategy and roadmap are approved, we execute the agreed initiatives. This includes improving existing assets and launching new campaigns in line with defined objectives, priorities, and performance benchmarks.

  4. 4

    Refinement

    Performance is reviewed continuously against agreed metrics. Learnings are applied iteratively to refine execution, improve effectiveness, and ensure marketing activity delivers consistent, commercially relevant results over time.

Your Marketing Delivery Team

Who’s in your marketing team?

One of our managing partners will head your project team & will guarantee you get the great marketing project you need. Depending on the scope-of-work in your brief, your delivery team may include any, or all, of the following:

Account Lead

Your Account Lead is your main point of contact and the person responsible for keeping delivery of your digital marketing on track. They coordinate the delivery team, manage weekly communication, gather your feedback, and ensure priorities, approvals, and timelines are aligned with the agreed scope.

Fractional Marketing Director

Executive-level marketing guidance to align strategy, execution, and reporting, without the cost of a full-time hire — allowing internal teams to focus on core operations.

Content Strategist – Copywriter

Your Content Strategist develops the messaging for your campaigns, websites, and sales materials. Their role is to present your offers in language your target audience understands, and responds to.

Marketing Collateral Designers

Your designer creates the visual assets needed to support campaigns and commercial communication, and helps maintain consistency, clarity, and professional presentation across your client-facing touchpoints. This may include landing pages, websites, video reels, social posts, presentations, handouts, digital ads, and other branded materials.

Performance Marketing Specialist

Your Performance Marketing Specialist is responsible for campaign setup, audience targeting, budget control, creative coordination, and performance optimisation across channels such as Google Ads and Reddit.

SEO Specialist

Your SEO specialist works to improve visibility with buyers actively researching solutions like yours. Their work focuses on keyword targeting, on-page improvement, content alignment, and search performance development to support qualified inbound demand over time.

Email Marketing and Lead Nurturing Specialist

This role builds and manages targeted email outreach campaigns that keep stakeholders engaged across longer B2B buying cycles. Their focus is to identify, validate, and structure target account data, and help move prospects from early interest to qualified sales conversation through timely, relevant communication.

Managing Partner Oversight

To maintain delivery quality and accountability, your project remains under director or managing partner supervision. Senior leadership joins scheduled review and strategy discussions, supports decision-making where needed, and helps ensure the overall engagement stays commercially focused and professionally managed.

We commit to support your marketing goals with pragmatic strategies, honest communications, and effective deliverables.

How is VIMI different from a typical digital marketing agency?

VIMI is structured to serve as your B2B marketing partner. We emphasize defined monthly scopes, weekly coordination, director-level oversight, and reporting tied to commercial outcomes.

What kinds of business outcomes do these marketing services support?

These marketing services are designed to help you reach and engage relevant buyers through a mix of short-term, mid-term, and longer-term strategies.

What services are included in the digital marketing project and how quickly should we expect to see results?

Your ROI’s and timeline depend on the strategies and benchmarks agreed for your project:

  • Fast ROI Campaigns – Immediate to 90 Days
    • Targeted paid campaigns
    • Press release distribution
    • Target account lead data
  • Mid-Term ROI Marketing – 3 to 6 Months
    • Short-form video reels
    • Strategic B2B content and copywriting
    • Email marketing and lead nurturing
  • Long-Term ROI Strategies – 6 to 12 Months
    • SEO
    • Executive LinkedIn support
  • Marketing Infrastructure and Support
    • Marketing and graphic design
    • Website operations and hosting
    • Fractional marketing leadership
How does VIMI decide what to prioritize first?

Your project begins with a discovery and benchmarking process to review your website performance and traffic, conversion rates, inquiry quality, competitive landscape, and current marketing gaps. Prioritization is then split across short-, mid-, and long-term opportunities so that early wins can be pursued without losing sight of longer-term pipeline and brand development.

Who will we work with, and how involved does our internal team need to be?

Your project delivery centers on a VIMI project manager who is assigned to your account. They are supported by specialists (e.g. copywriters, designers, performance marketers, SEO specialists, etc.). Your project manager reports to you weekly, and their performance is closely monitored by a managing partner. From your side, we expect timely input, access to briefing information, participation in weekly updates, and monthly review sessions.
